Measurement error: attenuation bias

When the predictor X is measured with error, ordinary least squares (OLS) underestimates the true slope. The naive estimate converges to β·λ, where λ = 1/(1+σ²) is the attenuation factor and σ is the noise standard deviation. Drag the slider to see the effect.
